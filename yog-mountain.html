Yog Mountain

A book of poetry and memoirs by S.S. Shankar that annotates each painting collection. "Rivers to Sea" - first published in 1981.

"Yog Mountain - Rivers to Sea" is a collection of poetry and personal memoirs that provides the philosophical and spiritual context for the visual art presented in Shankar Gallery.

Through verse and prose, Shankar explores the same themes that manifest in his paintings: the journey of the soul, the infinite void, the creative force of the universe, and the path of liberation through artistic practice.

The writings serve as companions to the visual works, offering deeper understanding of the spiritual traditions that inspire each series - from the sacred geography of Yatra to the meditative geometry of Yantra, from the cosmic emptiness of Maha Kala to the self-inquiry of Advaita.

Chapters

  • On Lingam - The Creative Force
  • Yatra - The Pilgrimage
  • Yantra - Sacred Geometry

  • Maha Kala - The Great Time
