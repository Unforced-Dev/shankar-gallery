Asian Art and Mysticism Meet Western Innovation

Welcome to Shankar Gallery, where S.S. Shankar, a Yogi in the Rocky Mountains, showcases his vibrant artwork, his deeply soulful writings, and his attentive skill as Siva Lingam Pujari.

These 108 visionary paintings explore a philosophy as old as Mount Kailash and culturally ancient as Arunachala Mountain. The collections feature holy temples, spiritual teachers, and abstract explorations of divine concepts in both representational and abstract styles.

Lingam Series

A celebration of the ultimate creative force in the universe. This foundational collection invites viewers to explore primal cosmic energy.

Yatra Series

Representational and abstract styles depicting spiritual destinations. Guides seekers on a colorful, visual pilgrimage to the holiest cities and temples on earth, including Varanasi, Nepal's Pasupati, and Chitrakoot.

Yantra Series

Multi-dimensional images that invite meditation and compel you to contemplate your own place in the universe.

Mandala Series

Inspired by ancient circular meditation objects. Go within and discover the Buddha who resides in each of us.

Maha Kala Series

Expresses the infinite void. Swirling vortexes of color and sparkling lights convey the almost incomprehensible expanse of total time and space.

Maha Samadhi Series

Six-by-ten-foot canvases presenting great awareness, confronting viewers with the awesome presence for which there are no words.

Sumi-e Doors Series

66x38-inch paintings using energetic bursts of color and spontaneous brush strokes to open into the limitless expanse of life.

Advaita Miniatures

Modeled on "Who am I?" these detailed works function as worlds in a microcosm, prompting self-inquiry.

Nada Series

Combining spray paint, opulent pastels, and delicate sumi-e brush strokes, these pieces capture the soft, ambient vibrations of the universe.

Yog Mountain

A book of poetry and memoirs by S.S. Shankar that annotates each painting collection.
